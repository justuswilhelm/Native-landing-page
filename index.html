About Habitat

Habitat connects creators with supporters through collaborative networks managed by trusted curators.
With Habitat, anyone can get involved in cryptocurrencies and token launches, share their experience, or gather support to launch their great idea.

Intuitive

Habitat makes it easy to participate in blockchain projects through an intuitive process.

Collaborative

Networks can easily collaborate, share information, and find opportunities using Habitat.

Open

Being blockchain agnostic, Habitat enables diversity across the spectrum of projects and ideas.

Trusted

Access the best opportunities and collaborative networks built by trusted curators.
